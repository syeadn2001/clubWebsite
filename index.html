Coordinated Engineering Program

The Brooklyn College Coordinated Engineering Program offers courses of study that are equivalent to the first two years at engineering schools. The program is small and provides a supportive environment for students. On completion of the Program, these students have transferred to various engineering schools such as Johns Hopkins University, NYU-Tandon School of Engineering, Stony Brook University, University of Wisconsin, University of Michigan, Cooper Union University of Buffalo, Grove School of Engineering at City College, and the College of Staten Island Engineering Science program to complete the remainder of their undergraduate program in engineering.
